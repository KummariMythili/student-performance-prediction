🧠 Understanding the Fields

  • Study Time: Number of hours the student studies per week. More study time can indicate better preparation and performance.
  • Failures: Number of past class failures. Higher failures may correlate with lower academic success.
  • Absences: Total number of school absences. Frequent absences may negatively affect performance.
  • G1 (First Grade): The student’s grade in the first period. It's an early performance indicator.
  • G2 (Second Grade): The student’s grade in the second period. Strongly predictive of the final outcome.
